Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how CollectorStream uses cookies and similar technologies. By using our service, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

CollectorStream uses cookies for:

  • Keeping you logged in to your account
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use our service
  • Improving our website and app performance

Important: We do NOT use cookies for advertising or tracking you across other websites.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for login and basic functionality Session or 30 days
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences (theme, language) 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how you use the app 2 years

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function:

  • auth_token - Keeps you logged in
  • session_id - Maintains your session
  • csrf_token - Protects against security attacks

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • user_preferences - Remembers your settings
  • theme - Remembers dark/light mode preference

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us improve:

  • _ga - Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • usage_stats - Feature usage tracking (anonymized)

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use minimal third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics - Website analytics (anonymized data only)
  • CloudFront - Content delivery for faster loading

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking

5. How to Control Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Mobile App

In our mobile app, cookies are used minimally for authentication only. You can manage app data through your device settings.

Opt-Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics:

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser

6. What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies:

  • You may not be able to log in
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some features may not work properly

Essential cookies are required for the service to function.

7. Cookie Retention

Cookies are retained based on their type:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Stored for the duration specified (up to 2 years)

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Sending an email notification (for major changes)

9. Your Consent

By continuing to use CollectorStream, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by disabling cookies in your browser.
